Tech Tips & Computer Care

Expert advice to keep your computer running smoothly

5 Signs Your Laptop Needs Repair

  • Slow startup and frequent freezing
  • Overheating and loud fan noise
  • Blue screen errors (BSOD)
  • Battery draining quickly
  • Strange noises from hard drive

If you notice these signs, contact us for free diagnosis!

How to Speed Up Your PC

  • Remove unnecessary startup programs
  • Clean temporary files regularly
  • Update drivers and software
  • Add more RAM if needed
  • Replace HDD with SSD

Need help? We offer PC optimization service for ₹500!

Protect Your PC from Viruses

  • Install reliable antivirus software
  • Keep Windows updated
  • Don't click suspicious links
  • Backup important data regularly
  • Use strong passwords

Already infected? Our virus removal starts at ₹300!

DIY Computer Maintenance

  • Clean keyboard and screen weekly
  • Keep vents dust-free
  • Restart your PC regularly
  • Check for Windows updates
  • Monitor hard drive space

For professional cleaning, book our service!

When to Replace Your Hard Drive

  • Frequent clicking or grinding sounds
  • Files getting corrupted
  • Extremely slow file access
  • Blue screen with disk errors
  • Drive not detected in BIOS

We offer data recovery and HDD replacement!

Prevent Laptop Overheating

  • Use laptop on hard, flat surfaces
  • Clean air vents monthly
  • Don't block ventilation holes
  • Consider a cooling pad
  • Monitor CPU temperature

Overheating issues? We fix cooling systems!
